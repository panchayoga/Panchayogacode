DVE/BHOOMI

Level: Intermediate & Experienced Beginners

3:30 - 4:00 AM.| 5:15 - 5:45 AM.| IST.

Monday to Saturday

Prana refers to 'breath' and Ayama means 'to control'. This 30-min controlled breathing session is designed to help you clear your mind’s clutter and start your day with a clean slate. As you slowly bring awareness to your breath, you become free of distractions and achieve inner alignment. In this tranquil state of mind, students are encouraged to set an intention and channel their energy towards their goals. With consistent pranayama and meditation practice, you will experience a number of benefits such as increased immunity, improved sleep quality, peaceful emotional healing and happiness. It can also help mitigate chronic conditions such as asthma, migraine, and gastric issues. Join us in the seat of silence as we exhale negativity, inhale positivity and maximize our potential for greatness.
